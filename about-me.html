About Me

Why I started the 30 Minute Dine

I started the 30 minute dine because I was fed up with how long it took to cook a nice meal. I kept going in a cycle of coming home exhausted or had to get work done but still wanting a high quality meal - only to realize that quality meals would take hours of prep time or a long wait at the restaurant I thought “why can't this just be quick”; that's when I wasn't the only one tired of eating out of a greasy takeout bag whenever I didn't want to cook that’s when I thought about a personal chef service that can cook you whatever you want without wasting time. A service that can strip away the time sink and annoyances of gourmet eating while still offering the quality dining of a high end restaurant.

My Mission and Ultimate Goal

The ultimate goal of The 30 Minute Dine is to reclaim the "lost evening." For too long, we’ve accepted that a fast dinner has to be a compromise and a great dinner has to be an event. Our mission is to flip that script by bridging the gap between the convenience of fast food and the soul of fine dining. We exist to strip away all time sinks of gourmet eating: the reservations, the travel, and the kitchen mess, and leave behind only the best part: incredible food.

We are built on three undebatable pillars: Uncompromising Quality, Unstoppable Speed, and Total Transparency. We believe that everyone deserves access to fresh, vibrant ingredients and professional culinary technique, regardless of how busy their calendar looks. We aren't just delivering a meal; we’re delivering a premium experience that respects your time. Our goal is a world where you can close your laptop at 6:00 PM and be sitting down to a chef-prepared, restaurant-grade meal by 6:30 PM. We don’t just feed people; we give them their nights back.
